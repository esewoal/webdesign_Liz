The Scroll Bar

A Brief History

A scrollbar is an interactive technique on a web browser which allows text, pictures, or any other content can be scrolled in a predetermined direction.

That is, up
down
left
or right .

It was first introduced in the late 1900s and since its conception, companies such as Xenox Star, Apple and Microsoft have developed it and adapted it to their various operating systems.
Its main purpose is to allow computer users to navigate their web windows effectively.
It was also known as a handle in the very first GUIs.
Although the scrollbar has gone through some changes since its conception, some things have stayed true to its design It consists of a hollow rectangular bar containing another solid bar
